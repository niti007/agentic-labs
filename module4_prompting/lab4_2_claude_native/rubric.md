# Lead scoring rubric — what the 0-10 score means

Plain guide so the score is meaningful (and consistent), not a gut feeling.

- **9-10 (hot):** clear buying intent — booked a demo, asked about pricing/contracts, big
  company, decision-maker.
- **6-8 (warm):** real interest but not ready — engaging with emails/content, mid-size,
  exploring.
- **3-5 (lukewarm):** mild interest, early days, or small/unclear fit.
- **0-2 (cold):** no real buying intent — students, job seekers, "just curious," no budget.

One short `reason` line should say *why* that number, in the customer's own signals.
