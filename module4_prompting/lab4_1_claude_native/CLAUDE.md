# Lab 4.1 (Claude-native) — precision prompting rules

This lab is about **how you ask**, not about code. The lesson: vague instructions get
vague, inconsistent answers; **explicit rules + a few worked examples** get sharp,
consistent ones.

When sorting customer feedback in this folder:
- Follow the urgency definitions in `criteria.md` exactly.
- Match the one-line format and calm tone shown in `examples.md`.
- For anything not spelled out, apply the *spirit* of the rules (e.g. a privacy/security
  issue is CRITICAL). If it could be two levels, pick the higher one.
