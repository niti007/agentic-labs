# Worked examples — the exact format and tone we want

These three labeled examples "show, don't tell." They lock in **the format** (one tidy
line), **the tone** (calm, factual), and **how to apply the criteria**. Give these to the
AI and every answer comes back looking the same.

**Output format (one line per message):**
`SEVERITY | one-line reason | suggested first action`

---

**Message:** "I've been double-charged $400 this month and nobody has replied. My lawyer
will be in touch if this isn't fixed today."
**Answer:** `CRITICAL | wrong charge + legal threat | refund immediately and call the customer`

**Message:** "The export button hasn't worked for two days, so I can't send my weekly
reports. Really need this fixed."
**Answer:** `MAJOR | core feature broken, blocking their work | assign to support today, confirm a timeline`

**Message:** "Loving the new dashboard! Any chance you'll add a dark mode?"
**Answer:** `MINOR | positive feedback + feature request | thank them and log the idea`
