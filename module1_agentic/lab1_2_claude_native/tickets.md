# Support tickets (Demo E — adaptive triage)

Each ticket should take a **different** path through `/triage`. Run them one at a time:
`/triage <paste one ticket>`.

## Ticket 1 — clear double-charge (expect: fast → issue_refund → resolve)
> I was charged twice for my subscription this month. Same plan, two identical
> $29.00 charges on the 3rd. Order IDs #A1021 and #A1022. Please refund one.

## Ticket 2 — vague complaint (expect: ask_clarifying_question first)
> Your app isn't working and I'm not happy about it. Fix it.

## Ticket 3 — threat / legal (expect: escalate_to_human immediately)
> This is the third time you've billed me wrongly. My lawyer will be contacting you
> and I'm filing a complaint with my bank and the regulator today.
